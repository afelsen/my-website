ZooML

1st Place Overall
Best Machine Learning Hack by the Raymond Corporation
Best Building a Better Future Hack by AIS
Best Civic Engagement Hack by J.P. Morgan

For the first ever virtual HackBU, it was only fitting to make a "Zoom" themed hack. My 3 teammates and I created an overlay for Zoom that detected and analyzed the facial expressions of individuals in a Zoom room.

• We used OpenCV for facial detection
• Implemented a custom made CNN in PyTorch to classify facial expressions ("talking", "attentive", "inattentive", "confused") trained on images of ourselves
• Created final interface with Tkinter

It came down to the wire this time, but we managed to submit our project and demo with a few minutes to spare. We are especially proud of the work we put into this project, and thrilled to receive 4 awards!

A Candidate Like You

Featured Project

With the presidential primaries on the way (in February 2020), our team developed a website that matches users with the presidential candidate they are most alike:

1. Convolutional Neural Network (CNN): Take a selfie to find which candidate looks most like you.
2. ML Chatbot: Have a candid conversation with our chatbot to get matched with a similar-minded candidate.
